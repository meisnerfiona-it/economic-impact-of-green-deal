# 📊 Economic Impact of the European Green Deal

## Energy Prices, Inflation & Interest Rates
### 📌 Overview

This project explores how energy price dynamics influence inflation across key EU economies (Germany, Spain, France, Italy), within the context of the **European Green Deal**.

We focus on identifying **patterns, persistence**, and **cross-country differences** in inflation behavior.

---

### 🎯 Key Questions
How do energy prices impact inflation?

Do these effects vary across countries?

Are inflation shocks temporary or persistent?

How does monetary policy react to inflation changes?
___

### 🔎 Hypothesis

**H1:** Changes in energy prices are positively associated with changes in inflation across EU countries.

**H2:** Inflation shocks are persistent over time rather than short-lived.

**H3:** The relationship between energy prices and inflation varies across countries.
